02 Meet your spaceships

Europa

Clipper

In order to conduct a thorough exploration of Europa, a world that has strong indications that an ocean of liquid water exists beneath its frozen cover and that might harbour ideal circumstances for life, the project will send a spacecraft into orbit around Jupiter. For repeated close flybys of the icy moon, the mission will place a highly competent, radiation-tolerant spacecraft into a lengthy, looping orbit around Jupiter.
