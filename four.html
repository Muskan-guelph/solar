Orion

The next exploration spacecraft from NASA, called Orion, is built to take astronauts farther into space than they've ever gone before, to places like Mars and asteroids. Orion will act as the crew's spacecraft for exploration, offering emergency abort capabilities, crew resupply during spaceflight, and safe re-entry from deep space return velocities. The Space Launch System, a potent new heavy-lift rocket from NASA, will carry Orion into space.
