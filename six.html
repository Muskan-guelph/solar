02 Meet your spaceships

TITAN

HUYGENS

Huygens was like the ultimate space traveler, hitching a ride with the Cassini spacecraft to Saturn and then braving the intense pressure and freezing temperatures of the moon Titan to give us a glimpse of its mysterious surface. This little probe was a true pioneer, transmitting the first images and data from the moon of another planet, making us all feel like armchair explorers. Plus, it's just fun to say "Huygens."
